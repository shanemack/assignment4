Home

For the final project I will create a website that provides examples of my writing, along with content that informs people of the reason behind my passion for, and style of writing. My website will be a way to show people my capabilities as a writer, while also connecting with them on a more personal level through original, engaging, and enlightening content.

The reason a professional or business may want to visit my website is if they are seeking a new, fresh writer with the ability to create writing material for diverse purposes. A professional/business can use my website as a way to learn more about my writing skill and style, through my brief insights of myself, and examples of my previous work. My website also provides an avenue of communication for them to contact me to further discuss their interests in having me write for them or for clarification of the structure in which I work with my clients.

Intro

As a freelance writer, I am always working on writing new content that can be applied to different industries and featured in different forms of writing. A publishing company may come across my website to explore my writing portfolio and learn more about me as a writer.

About Me

My website is primarily to market myself as a writer, but also to build a relationship with others and allow them to connect with my writing on a deeper level. Young adults may have interest in exploring my website because they want to read some of my writing that speaks on topics they find relevant or interesting.
